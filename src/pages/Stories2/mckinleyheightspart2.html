McKinley Heights Part 2

There was the traditional corner general store, run by the Seitz family, then Blatniks, then Kimmins.

On the Southwest corner is another one of the few continuous businesses in our area. It has been in operation since 1924. It started when Leonard Higley began selling plants raised in two small green houses on his property.

Stambaugh Thompson Company has a very busy and convenient hardware business.

Another old landmark is the red, white, and blue hat that is the home of the Army and Navy Garrison 422. It was formerly the Hat–O–Mat.

Going west of Route 422 within the boundaries of McKinley Heights is a convenience store, Heights Superette.

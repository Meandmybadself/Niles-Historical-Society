Niles-Alliance Railroad Bridge

Niles-Alliance railroad covered bridge over the Mahoning River. It was built in the late 1880's. Later the bridge became part of the Pennsylvania Railroad.

This historic bridge was incorporated into part of the Niles Bike Trail.

M.E. Riley bought the Harrison School in 1965 and turned it into a Technical School, teaching Drafting, Air Conditioning, Refrigeration, and Electronics.

On the Southeast Corner was a gas station, built in 1924 by Phil Bianco. It was operated as a Cities Service Station until it became a Fleet Wing station in 1947.

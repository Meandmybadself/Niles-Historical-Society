Benjamin F. Pew

The home of B. F. Pew and later of his daughter, Mrs. Edna Caldwell.

For the next ten years he was in the livery business and in 1884, thirty-six years ago, engaged in the retail grocery business, which he has continued to the present time. Mr. Pew was a grocer in Niles for 62 years.

The Jennings house on the corner was one of the first three houses built in 1894-1895.

Trumbull New Theatre

Trumbull New Theatre is located near the Eastwood Mall on Route 422 in Niles, Ohio.

Views of one of the early TNT plays

Close-up of one of the characters in an early play presented at the Pendleton house.

The first phase of the TNT Playhouse which cost $25,000 was completed in 1957. It included the stage, auditorium, and foyer. The mortgage was burned on this first step in June 1960. Pictured are: Richard Boyd, Marcia Russ, Carson, George Smith and TNT President, Stan Hollingsworth.

St. Luke Episcobal Church

Cover of the history of St. Luke’s Episcopal Church pamphlet published May 1970 in recognition of its 100th year celebration, 1870-1970.

On Nov. 11, 1870, the first Episcopal service was held in Niles by the Rev. Henry L. Badger of Warren and the Rev. Geo. S. Vallandigham of New Lisbon, Ohio.

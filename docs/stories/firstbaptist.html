The History of the First Baptist Church in Niles Ohio

Image of Welsh baptist Church.

Ground was broken in March 1892 and a frame church building was erected at 26 East Church Street and completed that same year. The following year a parsonage was built adjacent to the church building. This building was used as the Pastor’s home until 1933 after which it served as a Sunday School Annex.

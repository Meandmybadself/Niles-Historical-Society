Old Home Week 1909.

Niles Home Week was celebrated August 22-23 as shown in this pamphlet.

There are many hometown business advertisements shown in the pamphlet.

Thurday–Airship Ascensions, shows, and concessions.

Friday–Trumbull County Day, Parade at 1:30.

Saturday–Mardi Gras Parade, Devil Valear Death-Defying Act.

Jefferson Elementary School

The South Bentley Avenue Building built in 1911, was renamed Jefferson School in 1920, was closed in 1980 and razed. Jefferson Elementary School was yellow brick and had 18 rooms, but only 6 were used for classrooms. It also contained Music room, Nurse’s Room Lunch Room, and basement which was used for dances and “Get together” for young people

Senior housing apartments are now built on this site.

Students living on the south side of Robbins Avenue attended grades K-6.

This school was torn down after much controversy in the 1980's. PO1.1215

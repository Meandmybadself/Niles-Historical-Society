McKinley Heights Memories Part 3

Blotts

At the northern boundary of McKinley Heights, which is the Niles-Vienna Road, we have a farm that has been operated as a business for 65 years by the same family. First it was an orchard, then a poultry farm, then a dairy farm. It is presently run as a truck farm with an outlet that sells garden supplies, statuary items, fruits, vegetables, and plants.

Aerial view, looking east and south, of the intersection of State Route 422 and Niles-Vienna Road taken in 1973. Blott’s Farm Market, family house and fields are centered in the photograph.

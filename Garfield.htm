Garfield Elementary School.

Built in 1905, Third Street School was located at 103 West Third Street and first housed 94 students. The Third Street Building, renamed Garfield School in 1920, was the oldest continuing school building until its closure in 2003.

The building was sold in 2005 and was razed (2019).

The school was boarded up after being sold in 2005 and it was razed in 2019.
